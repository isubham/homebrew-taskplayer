Now-playing widget — 4 directions

Same live data feeding all four. Use the controls to compare how each handles a long title, pause state, and volume.

try the long title — that's what the marquee is for

1 · Cleaned original

Closest to what you have now, just tightened: no "Focus music" label, marquee title, flat skip + volume icons, genre shrunk to a small pill.

2 · Spotify twin

Two-line meta like Spotify's real now-playing corner — title on top (marquee), artist + genre tag below in muted text. Volume slider hides until you hover it.

3 · Capsule

Everything about the track — equalizer dot, marquee title, genre switcher — lives inside one rounded capsule (closer to the pill shape in your screenshot). Transport controls sit outside it.

4 · Ultra compact

No album-art tile at all — just an inline note glyph and marquee text. Volume becomes a click-to-open popover instead of a fixed-width slider, so the whole thing takes the least horizontal space.
